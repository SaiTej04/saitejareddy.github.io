About Me

Engineering with Impact

I'm a Senior Software Engineer with 5+ years of experience building large-scale, fault-tolerant backend systems across financial services. My work sits at the intersection of distributed systems engineering and regulatory compliance — domains where correctness and reliability are non-negotiable.

At Goldman Sachs, I architect and own backend services for the firm's global KYC and Client Onboarding platform — systems that process high-volume due-diligence workflows for clients across multiple business divisions worldwide. This means designing for correctness first, then scale, then speed.

I believe great engineering is invisible: systems that simply work, APIs that are a joy to consume, and services that teams can confidently extend. I bring that discipline to every codebase I touch — from schema design to Kafka topology to CI/CD pipelines.

My MS in Computer Science from the University of Central Missouri gave me the theoretical grounding; Goldman Sachs gave me the production reality. Both inform how I think.
